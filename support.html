Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?
Go to Settings → Restore Purchases inside the app. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
Why can't I edit my predictions?
Predictions lock 5 minutes before kickoff for each match. Once locked, only score and lineup entries can be added after the match ends.
How do I share a bracket?
Open the Bracket tab → tap the share icon in the top right. ChampsPath generates an image you can share to any app.
Will my data sync across devices?
Currently all data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. Cloud sync via iCloud is on the roadmap.
How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?
Send an email to erders61@gmail.com with steps to reproduce, your iOS version, and a screenshot if possible.
